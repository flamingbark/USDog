3/**
4 * Liquidation end-to-end test:
5 * 1) Detect if a vault (USER, ILK) is unsafe (ink*spot < art*rate)
6 * 2) If not unsafe, optionally force-unsafe by increasing mat via Spot.file and Spot.poke (requires wards on Spot)
7 * 3) Trigger liquidation via Dog.bark(ILK, USER, KPR) and fetch auction id
8 * 4) Verify Clipper has an active auction for this ILK by querying Dog.ilks(ILK).clip and Clipper.list()
9 *
10 * Optional 5) Place a partial bid with Clipper.take() using internal dai:
11 * - Join external USDog via DaiJoin.join to create internal dai
12 * - Call Clipper.take with max price and a small amt
13 *
14 * Usage:
15 * ILK=SHIB-A USER=0xYourVaultOwner KPR=0xYourKeeperAddress FORCE=1 BID=0.0 TAKE_MAX=999999999999999999999 npx hardhat run scripts/test-liquidations.js --network bsc
16 *
17 * Notes:
18 * - FORCE=1 tries to set a very high mat (e.g., 500% RAY) then spot.poke(ILK) to make the position unsafe.
19 * - BID & TAKE_MAX are optional to try purchasing collateral from the auction (requires internal dai).
20 */
21
